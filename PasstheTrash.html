Rules

$25 Buy In: three $5 piles and one $10 lifeline. Each round every active player receives one card face down.

Pass phase

  • Starting left of the dealer, each player either keeps their card or swaps it with the player on their left.
  • If a pass target holds a King, the King flips face up and blocks the pass.
  • The dealer, when reached, may keep or draw from the deck.
  • Used cards go to a discard pile. If there are not enough cards left to start a full round, a fresh deck is shuffled.

Lifelines

  • Once per game, a player may use their lifeline.
  • Three lifeline uses: take a flipped King; prevent someone from passing to you; draw a replacement card after the round's reveal.
  • You may not use a lifeline to answer another lifeline use.

Paying

  • After replacement (lifeline) decisions, the lowest card rank (Ace is lowest) pays one $5 pile into the pot. Tied low cards each pay.
  • If no one attempts to pass, and the dealer does not draw, "Bullshit from Bananas" applies and each loser pays TWO piles.
  • When a player loses all three piles, they are out. Unused lifelines are returned to their stack.
  • Last player with piles wins the pot.
