Rules

Goal: decide if your 3-card hand is strong enough to stay vs the table — or against the Monster if all others fold.

Hand Flow

  • Each player receives 3 cards. The Monster receives 4.
  • Players secretly choose Stay or Fold. CPU decisions are revealed after you choose.
  • If everyone folds, the best folded hand pays the Hoard a coward tax. Each player's first tax is 5% of their stack, rising by 1% each time they pay it, to a maximum of 15%.
  • If exactly one player stays, that player must face off vs the Monster.
  • If two or more players stay, highest hand takes money from the Hoard and the losers pay into it.

Monster and caps

  • If awoken to face a single opponent, the Monster uses the best 3 out of its 4 cards, AND it's 2's are wild.
  • Player loss cap is controlled by the slider, a percent of current stack size.
  • Payouts from the Hoard are capped at 10 times the loss cap. For example, if you risk 5% of your stack, you can win 50%.
  • When the Hoard is gone, every player antes $1 to seed the next Hoard.
